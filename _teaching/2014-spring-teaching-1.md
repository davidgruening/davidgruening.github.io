Teaching the statistical parts of the Social Cognition Lab, including: </br>  
Introduction to Expert Theory </br> 
Introduction to R and JASP </br> 
Non-parametric testing: Wilcoxon test, Mann Whitney-U test and Conover-Iman test </br> 
Introduction to Bayesian statistics </br> 
Bayesian analyses in psychological research </br> 
Exploratory factor analysis </br> 
Additional analysis-session to advance students’ research-project analyses </br> 

& 

Supervision of student research projects: </br>  
 Project 1: Rolling a die vs. thinking - what to prefer? </br> 
 Project 2: Ultimatum game - rationality and morality </br> 
 Project 3: Five group projects, </br> 
		1. COVID & priming </br>
		2. Cognitive load’s impairment for anchor effects </br>
		3. IAT on stereotypes caused by COVID </br>
		4. The ultimatum game and primes of danger </br>
		5. Donating in times of COVID </br>
